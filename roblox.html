Roblox

Roblox is an online gaming platform that allows players to create and share their games. It features a wide variety of games created by users, ranging from simple obstacle courses to complex simulations. Players can customize their avatars, socialize with others, and participate in various in-game events. Roblox is known for its creative community and has gained immense popularity among players of all ages.
